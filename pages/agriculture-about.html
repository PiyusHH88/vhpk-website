About Us

Our Farm & Mission

VHPK Energy Pvt. Ltd. is a multifaceted business with an emphasis on innovation and sustainability. The company, which is based in Himachal Pradesh and Delhi, is mostly involved in organic farming, with a focus on growing Sweat Lemon, curry leaves and encouraging environmentally friendly gardening techniques. Planted in 100 acres area, regarding the organic varieties of Sweet Lemon (including Jafa, Satgudi, Blood Red, Malta, Balancia, and Mosambi), these citrus fruits are cultivated organically in Himachal Pradesh’s favorable mid and high-hill climatic zones. Each variety offers distinctive attributes: Jafa is aromatic and mild; Satgudi is juicy and sweet with low acidity; Blood Red is rich in antioxidants with tangy flavor; Malta presents a semi-sweet aroma-rich profile; Balancia is hardy with disease resistance; Mosambi is prized for its mild sweetness and detoxifying properties. Organic farming practices in the region emphasize chemical-free cultivation using compost, fermented organic manures, and natural pest control such as neem extracts, thus preserving fruit quality and health benefits. In addition to its agricultural endeavors, VHPK Energy provides goods and services in the railway sector, which aid in the development of India's infrastructure. Our goal is to develop India's green and engineering future while combining industrial advancement with natural prosperity to create value for communities.
